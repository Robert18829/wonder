1. Great Wall of China

Information about Great Wall of China:
We can see Great Wall of China from the space. This is 21196 km (13171 miles) long. It crosses 15 provinces and extends from Xinjiang to the border of Korea .
It was built during the Ming dynasty (between 1368 and 1644).
The Great Wall was extended to protect the Silk Road trade. The Ming dynasty is known for not only extending the Great Wall, but also repairing and reinforcing existing structures.
It was built by hand, though primitive technology — wheel barrows, ropes, basket-and-pulley systems, and horse- or oxen-drawn carts — was also used.
